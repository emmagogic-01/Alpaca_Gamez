Where's Walrus?

Find the cute green-eyed walrus hiding in each busy scene.

Level: 1
Score: 0
Time: 60s

How to Play

  • Look carefully through the scene.
  • Click the walrus when you find it.
  • Wrong clicks cost points.
  • Use hints if you get stuck.
  • The walrus may be tiny, tilted, or partly hidden behind objects.
  • There are 20 levels, and the later ones are intentionally crowded.

Tip: the walrus gets smaller and the scenes get busier each level.
